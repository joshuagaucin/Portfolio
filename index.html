Intro

I am Joshua Gaucin, my hope is to live life with as little regrets as possible,im always trying to improve myself and my situation. Everything happens for a reason which is probably why you're reading this right now! By the way, check out my awesome work.

Im the oldest child, I have seven siblings! I was born in Kansas City and I havent moved around much, hence the need for a remote job!
